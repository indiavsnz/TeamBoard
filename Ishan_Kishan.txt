Ishan Kishan is an Indian international cricketer who plays as a wicket-keeper batter. He is known for his aggressive batting style and quick scoring in limited-overs cricket.

Basic Information

Full Name: Ishan Pranav Kumar Pandey Kishan

Date of Birth: 18 July 1998

Birthplace: Patna, Bihar, India

Role: Wicketkeeper–Batsman

Batting Style: Left-handed

Bowling Style: Right-arm off-break (rarely bowls)


Domestic & IPL Career

Plays domestic cricket for Jharkhand.

In the Indian Premier League, he has mainly played for Mumbai Indians.

He was bought by Mumbai Indians for a very high price in the 2022 IPL auction.


International Career

Made his T20 International debut for India in 2021.

Also plays ODI cricket for India national cricket team.

Known for explosive starts as an opening batter.


Major Achievement

Scored 210 runs in an ODI against Bangladesh in 2022, which is:

The fastest double century in ODI cricket.

One of the highest individual scores in ODIs.



Interesting Facts

He captained the Indian team in the 2016 U-19 Cricket World Cup.

Famous for his fearless batting and powerful shots.

One of the few players to score a double century in ODI at a very young age.
