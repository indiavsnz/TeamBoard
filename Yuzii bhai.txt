Yuzvendra Chahal is an Indian international cricketer known for his leg-spin bowling. He is one of India’s most successful T20 and ODI spin bowlers.

Basic Information

Full Name: Yuzvendra Singh Chahal

Date of Birth: 23 July 1990

Birthplace: Jind, Haryana, India

Role: Bowler

Bowling Style: Right-arm leg break

Batting Style: Right-handed


International Career

Plays for the India national cricket team mainly in ODI and T20I formats.

Made his international debut in 2016.

Known for taking important wickets in the middle overs.


IPL Career

Plays in the Indian Premier League.

Has played for teams like Royal Challengers Bengaluru and Rajasthan Royals.

One of the top wicket-takers in IPL history.


Achievements

First Indian bowler to take a 6-wicket haul in T20 International cricket.

Consistently among the highest wicket-takers in IPL seasons.

Known for his smart variations and flighted leg-spin bowling.


Interesting Facts

Before cricket, he was a national-level chess player.

Famous for his funny personality and celebrations on the field.

One of the most successful leg-spinners in limited-overs cricket.
