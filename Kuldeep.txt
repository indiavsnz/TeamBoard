Kuldeep Yadav is an Indian international cricketer known for his left-arm wrist spin (chinaman bowling). His unique bowling style makes it difficult for batsmen to read the ball.

Basic Information

Full Name: Kuldeep Yadav

Date of Birth: 14 December 1994

Birthplace: Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, India

Role: Bowler

Bowling Style: Left-arm wrist spin (Chinaman)

Batting Style: Left-handed


International Career

Plays for the India national cricket team in Test, ODI, and T20I formats.

Made his international debut in 2017.

Known for taking wickets in the middle overs with spin variations.


IPL Career

Plays in the Indian Premier League.

Has played for teams like Kolkata Knight Riders and Delhi Capitals.

Known for taking important wickets with spin bowling.


Achievements

One of the few bowlers to take a hat-trick in ODI cricket for India.

Has taken many five-wicket hauls in international matches.

Part of the famous spin pair “Kul-Cha” (Kuldeep + Chahal) for India.


Interesting Facts

Initially wanted to become a fast bowler, but his coach advised him to become a spinner.

His chinaman bowling is rare in world cricket.

Played an important role in many India victories in limited-overs matches.
