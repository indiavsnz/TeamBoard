Ravindra Jadeja is one of the best all-rounders in world cricket. He is famous for his spin bowling, quick fielding, and useful batting, making him a complete player for India.

Basic Information

Full Name: Ravindrasinh Anirudhsinh Jadeja

Date of Birth: 6 December 1988

Birthplace: Jamnagar, Gujarat, India

Role: All-rounder

Batting Style: Left-handed

Bowling Style: Left-arm orthodox spin


International Career

Plays for the India national cricket team in Test, ODI, and T20I formats.

Made his international debut in 2009.

Known for his all-round performances and brilliant fielding.


IPL Career

Plays in the Indian Premier League.

A key player for Chennai Super Kings.

Famous for finishing matches with both bat and ball.


Achievements

One of the top all-rounders in ICC rankings.

Scored triple centuries in domestic cricket (Ranji Trophy) early in his career.

Known for match-winning spells and quick batting cameos.


Interesting Facts

Nicknamed “Sir Jadeja” by fans.

Famous for his sword celebration after scoring runs.

Considered one of the best fielders in world cricket.
