Rohit Sharma is one of the most successful batsmen and captains in world cricket. He is famous for his elegant batting style, pull shot, and big scores, which is why he is often called the “Hitman.”

Basic Information

Full Name: Rohit Gurunath Sharma

Date of Birth: 30 April 1987

Birthplace: Nagpur, Maharashtra, India

Role: Opening Batsman

Batting Style: Right-handed

Bowling Style: Right-arm off-break (occasionally)


International Career

Plays for the India national cricket team in Test, ODI, and T20I formats.

One of the most successful captains of India in limited-overs cricket.

Known for scoring very big innings in ODIs.


IPL Career

Plays in the Indian Premier League.

Former captain of Mumbai Indians.

Under his captaincy, Mumbai Indians won multiple IPL titles.


Major Achievements

Holds the record for the highest individual ODI score (264 runs).

The only player to score three double centuries in ODI cricket.

One of the highest run scorers in international cricket.


Interesting Facts

Famous for his effortless timing and pull shot.

Has scored many centuries in ICC tournaments.

Started his career as a middle-order batsman and later became a successful opener.
