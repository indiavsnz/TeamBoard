Suryakumar Yadav is an Indian international cricketer famous for his innovative batting style. He is often called “Mr. 360°” because he can play shots in all directions of the field.

Basic Information

Full Name: Suryakumar Ashok Yadav

Date of Birth: 14 September 1990

Birthplace: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India

Role: Top-order Batsman

Batting Style: Right-handed

Bowling Style: Right-arm medium (occasionally)


IPL Career

Plays in the Indian Premier League.

A key player of Mumbai Indians.

Known for scoring quick runs and playing creative shots.


International Career

Represents the India national cricket team mainly in T20 International cricket.

Made his international debut in 2021.

Became one of the top-ranked T20 batsmen in the world.


Achievements

One of the fastest players to score multiple T20I centuries for India.

Known for his 360-degree batting shots, similar to modern T20 legends.

Consistently ranked among the top T20I batsmen in ICC rankings.


Interesting Facts

Took many years in domestic cricket before getting his India debut.

Famous for shots like scoop, reverse sweep, and innovative lofted shots.


from pyspark.sql.functions import current_timestamp, lit
import json

HRMID = "HRM1111"

create schema if not exists exam.audit


CREATE TABLE IF NOT EXISTS exam.audit.hrm5896_audit_log (
  MessageDateAndTime TIMESTAMP,
  BatchID INT,
  MessageSource STRING,
  MessageType STRING,
  MessageText STRING,
  MessageData STRING,
  HRMID STRING
)
USING DELTA;



def write_audit_log(
    batch_id: int,
    message_source: str = None,
    message_type: str = None,
    message_text: str = None,
    message_data: dict = None,
    source: str = None
):
    final_source = message_source if message_source is not None else source
    data_json = json.dumps(message_data) if message_data else None

    audit_df = spark.createDataFrame(
        [(batch_id, final_source, message_type, message_text, data_json, HRMID)],
        ["BatchID", "MessageSource", "MessageType", "MessageText", "MessageData", "HRMID"]
    ).withColumn("MessageDateAndTime", current_timestamp())

    audit_df.createOrReplaceTempView("tmp_audit_log")

    spark.sql("""
        INSERT INTO exam.audit.hrm1111_audit_log
        SELECT
            MessageDateAndTime,
            BatchID,
            MessageSource,
            MessageType,
            MessageText,
            MessageData,
            HRMID
        FROM tmp_audit_log
    """)
