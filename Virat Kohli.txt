Virat Kohli is one of the greatest batsmen in modern cricket. He is known for his consistency, aggressive attitude, and excellent chasing ability, which is why he is often called the “King Kohli” and “Chase Master.”

Basic Information

Full Name: Virat Kohli

Date of Birth: 5 November 1988

Birthplace: Delhi, India

Role: Top-order Batsman

Batting Style: Right-handed

Bowling Style: Right-arm medium (rarely)


International Career

Plays for the India national cricket team in Test, ODI, and T20I formats.

Made his international debut in 2008.

Former captain of the Indian cricket team in all formats.


IPL Career

Plays in the Indian Premier League for Royal Challengers Bengaluru.

One of the most loyal players, playing for the same IPL team since 2008.


Major Achievements

One of the highest run scorers in international cricket.

Holds the record for most runs in a single IPL season (973 runs in 2016).

Scored many centuries in ODI and Test cricket.

Known for his fitness and intense playing style.


Interesting Facts

Led India to many important victories as captain.

Famous for his perfect cover drive shot.

Married to Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma.
